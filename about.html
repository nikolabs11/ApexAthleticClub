Pricing and Value

Apex Athletic Club offers a comprehensive year-round program at $200 per month. This monthly fee covers:

  • 12 months of multi-sport programming
  • Professional coaching from full-time staff
  • Access to facilities year round
  • Participation in leagues and tournaments
  • Cross-training in multiple sports for holistic athlete development
  • Private small group training sessions
